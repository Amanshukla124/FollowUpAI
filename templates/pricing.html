Frequently Asked Questions
What counts as one email?
Each time you click "Generate Email" counts as one email. Editing and copying the result does not use additional credits.
When do my free emails reset?
Free emails reset on the 1st of every calendar month. You get 3 fresh emails every month at no cost.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes, anytime. Go to My Account → Manage Billing to cancel. You'll keep Pro access until the end of the billing period.
Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. All payments are processed by Stripe — we never see or store your card details. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant.
What AI model powers FollowUpAI?
We use GPT-4o-mini via OpenRouter for fast, high-quality email generation at low cost — which is how we keep the price affordable.
